**Causal models for event types and event structure**  
Complex, bounded event types (e.g., accomplishments) link together simpler events, states, properties, and individuals/thematic roles with respect to their relationship to a goal or point of culmination. Type-level causal models provide a way of capturing the world knowledge that constitutes these types and feeding into lexical semantic structure in a way that interacts with (non-)culminating interpretations under modification by grammatical aspects and aspectual verbs. Joint work with [Elitzur Bar-Asher Siegal.](https://scholars.huji.ac.il/elitzurbarashersiegal/home)

*   2022.\[Workshop presentation.\] [Causal models are (not) about events.](pdfs/Bar-AsherSiegal-Nadathur-COCOA_events-handout.pdf) _Converging on Causal Ontology Analyses (COCOA)._ Oct 12.

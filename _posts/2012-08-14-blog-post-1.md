Understanding noun phrases (NPs) in the SOV language Hindi involves analyzing their structure, functions, and role within sentence syntax. In Hindi, noun phrases typically include a noun as the head, accompanied by modifiers such as adjectives, determiners, or quantifiers, with word order reflecting the head-final structure characteristic of SOV languages. This study examines the formal properties of Hindi NPs, their agreement patterns, and their interaction with verbs to contribute to syntactic and semantic theory.
